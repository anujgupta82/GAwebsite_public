Monetizing AI Products Takes 4 Roles Most Businesses Don’t Have

Data & AI Strategists

Data & AI Strategists create the top-level strategy for developing and monetizing data and AI. They control the data organization’s strategic direction and implement their vision for the technology.

Data & AI Product Managers

Data & AI Product Managers drive opportunity discovery and the data product strategy. They connect the business’s top-level technical strategy to execution and translate between technical and non-technical teams.

Strategic Data Leaders

Strategic Data Leaders develop the data organization to meet business needs. They control strategy implementation, lead innovation, and ensure execution. They implement their vision for the team and AI capabilities for the enterprise.

Value-Centric Data Professionals

Value-Centric Data Professionals deliver high-end solutions to the business’s most complex challenges. They translate business problems into data science solutions. They control the entire data science lifecycle and solutions delivery.

How The Courses Fit Together

I built the courses in 4 layers, each designed to target a different piece of the monetization challenge.

Data & AI Strategy Technical Certification teaches the C-level piece of the puzzle and is designed to train a data strategist. It teaches you how to build a strategy that will inform decision-making across the business about data and AI. This is your top-down monetization strategy, and it's connected with the core business and operating models.

Strategic Leadership Certification is built for data team and organizational leaders. It covers transitioning from a tactical manager to a strategic leader, data team development (individual and organizational), leading strategy implementation, and working with C-level leaders and the rest of the business.

Data Product Manager teaches the second layer, data and AI product strategy. This connects the top-level data and AI strategy to execution. The purpose is to train a data product manager to turn strategy into product and infrastructure roadmaps linked to business value.

The Value-Centric Data Professional Certification is the final layer. It’s designed to upskill data professionals with business acumen and an understanding of the monetization, commercialization, and productization processes. It teaches a data professional how to go from business cases to data solutions. The course has a heavy focus on communications frameworks and developing business acumen.
