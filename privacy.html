Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Gradient Advisors, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy informs you about the types of personal information we collect and how it may be used. This policy applies solely to our website www.gradient-advisors.ai.

Information We Collect and How It's Used

Information You Voluntarily Submit

We may collect personal information such as your name and email address when you voluntarily submit information, for example, by leaving a comment, subscribing to our newsletter, or submitting a contact form. If you create an account, we store your username but not your password.

Information from Other Sources

We may receive information about you from other services you use via our website. For instance, third-party platforms may transfer data to us for processing.

Automatically-Collected Information

We automatically collect data such as your IP address, browser type, and activity on the site, including pages viewed and times accessed. This helps us understand how users interact with the site.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience by storing session information. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but note that disabling cookies may affect the website’s functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information in the following ways:

  • To maintain and operate the website
  • To create your account and personalize your experience
  • To send promotional communications, such as newsletters, with an opt-out option
  • To send administrative information such as technical notices and updates
  • To respond to comments or inquiries
  • To measure and track advertising on the website
  • To process payments for purchases
  • To prevent and detect unauthorized or illegal activities

Third-Party Use of Personal Information

We may share your information with third-party service providers for website maintenance, advertising, and email delivery. These include:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and user activity.
  • ThriveCart: Manages our e-commerce platform.
  • ConvertKit: Handles email newsletters and updates.
  • Calendly: Manages appointment scheduling.

We do not sell or distribute your personal information without consent, except in cases of business acquisitions or legal obligations.

Anonymous Data

We may collect anonymous data, which doesn’t identify individual users, for marketing or advertising purposes. Examples include analytics data or cookie-collected information.

Cookies

We use cookies to track your preferences and improve your experience. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser. Note that disabling cookies might reduce the website’s functionality.

Advertising

We may use third-party ad services that employ cookies to display ads based on your browsing behavior. Retargeting efforts may also be employed through platforms like Google and Facebook.

Our affiliate marketing efforts may place cookies to track commissions if you purchase through affiliate links.

Rights Related to Your Personal Information

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from emails by following the instructions in the emails.
  • Access: Request access to your personal data by contacting us.
  • Amend: Request changes or corrections to your information.
  • Forget: Request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Please note that we may retain data as necessary for legal obligations or business purposes.

Children's Information

Our website does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@gradient-advisors.ai.
